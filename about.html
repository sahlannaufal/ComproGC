Company Information

PT. Green Cold

PT. Green Cold was founded in 2011. Over these years, we have focused on Cleanroom systems (including electronics, pharmaceuticals, food, and other industries). We also specialize in industrial equipment installation, mechanical and electrical engineering, central chiller water air conditioning systems, central monitoring systems, and cold storage engineering.
